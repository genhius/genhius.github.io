ABOUT ME

I am a visual artist who uses art as a mean of self-expression. I have a great passion for photography, drawing and digital art.

I am often inspired by the art of cinematography, books and events happening around me. My works focus on exploring the beauty of how we, as human beings, interpret the world around us and how we could use various types of art to convey our thoughts.
